Trip Overview

This itinerary captures the perfect spring window, combining the history of the North (Jerash & Ajloun) with the wonders of the South. You'll arrive just after Ramadan ends, meaning restaurants and sites are fully open.

Daily Itinerary

Sunday, March 29

Ahlan wa Sahlan (Welcome to Amman)

Arrival

Arrival at Queen Alia International Airport (AMM). Transfer to hotel (45 mins). Depending on arrival time, explore Rainbow Street for cafes and views.

Dinner Tip: Try Sufra on Rainbow Street for authentic Mansaf or Mezze.
Monday, March 30

Romans, Crusaders & The Citadel

History
  • Morning (Jerash): Drive north (45 mins). Explore the Roman city of Jerash (Biblical Gerasa).
  • Mid-Day (Ajloun): Visit Ajloun Castle, a 12th-century fortress built by Saladin's general, offering incredible views.
  • Afternoon (Amman): Return to Amman. Visit the Jordan Museum (history overview) and the Citadel (Jabal al-Qala'a) for sunset.
  • Evening: Walk downtown to see the Roman Theater and try 'Knafeh' at Habibah Sweets.
Tuesday, March 31

The Kings Highway to Petra

Road Trip

A scenic drive south (approx. 4-5 hours total driving time).

Stop 1: Madaba St. George’s Church & the Mosaic Map.
Mt. Nebo Where Moses viewed the Promised Land.
Stop 3: Kerak Castle A massive Crusader fortress with underground mazes.

Overnight in Wadi Musa (Petra).

Wednesday, April 1

Petra (Full Day & By Night)

Highlight

Biblical Sela. Start early (6:30 AM).

  • Walk the Siq to the Treasury (Al-Khazneh).
  • Explore Street of Facades & Royal Tombs.
  • Hike to the Monastery (Ad Deir).
Petra by Night: It's Wednesday! Return at 8:30 PM to see the Siq and Treasury lit by 1,500 candles.
Thursday, April 2

Little Petra to Wadi Rum

Morning: Visit "Little Petra" (Siq al-Barid).

Afternoon: Drive to Wadi Rum (2 hours). Check into a Bedouin camp.

Activity: 4x4 Jeep tour to see rock bridges, inscriptions, and red dunes at sunset.

Dinner: Enjoy traditional Zarb (Bedouin underground BBQ) and stargazing.
Friday, April 3

Desert Morning & The Red Sea

Morning: Optional camel ride at sunrise. Enjoy a traditional Bedouin breakfast.

Afternoon: Drive south to Aqaba (1 hour). Check into a beach resort. Spend the rest of the day relaxing by the pool or Red Sea.

Saturday, April 4

Aqaba Marine Life

Relaxation

Full day in Aqaba. The water temperature is perfect.

Snorkeling: Head to South Beach (Japanese Garden reef).
Diving: See the Cedar Pride wreck or military museum.
